Due Two Kettő

Carole Bloch

Richard MacIntosh

Laura Pighini

Sonia Pighini

Italian

Level 1

Due piccole mani per tenere.

Két kis kéz, hogy fogjon.

Two little hands to hold.

Due piccoli piedi per calciare.

Két kis láb, hogy rúgjon.

Two little feet to kick.

Due piccoli occhi per vedere.

Két kis szem, hogy lásson.

Two little eyes to see.

Due piccole orecchie per ascoltare.

Két kis fül, hogy halljon.

Two little ears to hear.

E due amorevoli braccia per ABBRACCIARE!

És két szerető kar, hogy megöleljen.

And two loving arms to HUG!

Written by: Carole Bloch
Illustrated by: Richard MacIntosh
Translated by: Laura Pighini
Read by: Sonia Pighini
Language: Italian
Level 1
Source: Two from African Storybook
